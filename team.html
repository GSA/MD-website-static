Our Team

Stephanie Anderson

Stephanie Anderson is an analyst who works mostly in a customer facing role. Most recently, Stephanie has been the Project Manager for the Mission-Support Benchmarking Initiative and the Motor Vehicle Fleet movement to Asset Level Data. She also has been leading the external communication and internal development and visualizations of the President’s Management Agenda Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that are currently published on the performance.gov website. Stephanie has been with GSA for almost 5 years, since October of 2014. When Stephanie is not at work, she enjoys traveling with her husband. Their latest trip was to Piura, Peru, to do missionary work. When she is not traveling, you can find her checking out breweries in Loudoun County with her Siberian Husky, Princess.

Scott McAllister

Scott is a data scientist, working mostly in Python on natural language processing problems. He's the lead developer for the Solicitation Review Tool and advises on anything related to machine learning. Prior to joining GSA, Scott was a Peace Corps Volunteer teaching English in Ethiopia. In his free time, he's a runner, reader, and amateur ludologist.

Aaron Eisenbarth

Aaron Eisenbarth is a Senior Data Management Advisor in the Office of Evidence and Analysis. He received his Master of Public Administration and Master of International Relations from the Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs at Syracuse University. Prior to GSA, he worked for the Governor of the state of Washington and served in The Republic of Moldova as a Peace Corps volunteer. He specializes in big data analysis and management with a focus on Real Property issues. In particular, he has worked to empower agency decision making through the merging, analysis, and visualization of datasets like the Federal Real Property Profile and tools the Real Property Management Tool, Asset Consolidation Tool, and Tenant Satisfaction Survey Tool.

Donnita Mennealy

Donnita is a SQL developer and analyst for the Office of Evidence and Analysis's database team. Donnita was the lead analyst for Mission Support Benchmarking Contracting initiative and has developed multiple visualization tools for GSA's acquisition community. In this role, Donnita's primary focus is on FPDS, FedScope, FEVS, and EHRI data specifically related to acquisition and human capital policy areas. Prior to this role, Donnita has served in PBS for over 13 years. During her time there, she served as a Realty Specialist, Program analyst for NCR's PBS Leasing Policy and Performance Division, Branch Chief for NCR's Business Analytics Branch, and contracting officer for commercial leasing.

Donnita studied at Kalamazoo College where she double-majored in Economics and Political Science. After graduation, she went on to receive her Master's Degree in Economics at American University's College of Arts and Sciences. In high school, Donnita developed a love for Japanese culture. She had the opportunity to study abroad in high school in northern Japan and in college where she attended Waseda University in Tokyo. When Donnita is not working, she is active in her local community, spending quality time with her husband and two children, travels to major league baseball stadiums, and collects Japanese stationery.

Scott is a data scientist and Python developer, working on whatever problem comes his way. Scott graduated from Virginia Tech with a degree in Economics and was recruited into the Emerging Leaders Program at GSA. In his free time he likes to woodwork and read.
