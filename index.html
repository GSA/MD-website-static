Using Modern Analytics Practices to Help Shape Policy

Technology and the Government are usually oxymorons. OGP's Office of Evidence and Analysis uses cutting-edge open source Data Science concepts such as Machine Learning, AI, and Serverless Design. We work in conjunction with policy makers to make more efficient and data driven public policies.

Tenant Satisfaction Survey

The Tenant Satisfaction Survey is developed collaboratively by OCE, PBS, and OGP.The tool itself, made in Tableau, offers insights into what drives satisfaction and dissatisfaction in a given building.

Solicitaion Review Tool

This project fetches solicitation data, parses it, and uses supervised machine learning to determine whether or not the solicitation notices contain appropriate setion 508 accessibility language.

OMB KPI's

The President’s Management Agenda identifies cross-agency priority (CAP) goals to target those areas where multiple agencies must collaborate to effect change and report progress in a manner the public can easily track.

Customer Satisfaction Survey

The Customer Satisfactino Survey is a government-wide survey conducted by the Office of Evidence and Analysis. It provides valuabel information to agaenices on the quality and satisfaction of delivery of support services.
