OMB KPIs

The President’s Management Agenda identifies cross-agency priority (CAP) goals to target those areas where multiple agencies must collaborate to effect change and report progress in a manner the public can easily track. There are several publically available key performance indicators that are used to measure how different goals are performing across agencies.
