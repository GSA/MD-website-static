Customer Satisfaction Survey (CSS)

The Customer Satisfactino Survey is a government-wide survey conducted by the Office of Evidence and Analysis. It provides valuabel information to agaenices on the quality and satisfaction of delivery of support services.

It measures performance of four key mission support functions: Contracting, Human Capital, Financial Management, and Information Technology
